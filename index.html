About

Doctor
Shahila Ameer

Dr Shahila is a Specialist Obstetrics and Gynaecology, delivering quality care to all women. She has over 20 years of experience in her field, including 10 years in UAE. She has extensive experience in management of all types of pregnancies, labor and delivery, and both surgical and medical management of gynecological disorders. She completed her MBBS from LUMHS Pakistan and started postgraduate training in Obstetrics and Gynaecology. She obtained the membership of the Royal College of Obstetrics and Gynaecology from London (UK) .She has been actively involved in training the postgraduate trainees and is certified examiner of MRCOG – UK.
